Study Tips That Work

1. Use short notes and flashcards. 2. Study in small blocks of time like 25 mins focus + 5 mins break. 3. Explain topics to yourself or your friends — it helps you remember better. 4. Stay away from distractions while studying.

Exam Preparation Guide

Start your revision at least 2 weeks before exams. Solve previous year papers. Focus on important topics first. Don’t panic — take it one step at a time. We also provide quick revision charts and sample questions to help you prepare easily.
